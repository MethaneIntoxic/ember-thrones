# UX Flow

## Primary Journey
1. Launch app.
2. Load profile and wallet.
3. Review jackpot ladder and feature status.
4. Adjust bet and spin.
5. View win/feature results and continue.

## Dragon-Style UX Rules
- Jackpot ladder remains visible at all times.
- Ember Lock and Free Quest status always shown in HUD.
- Spin interactions are responsive with visible reel inertia and settle rhythm.
- Bonus transitions are dramatic but not disruptive on mobile.

## Accessibility
- Reduced motion toggle.
- High contrast token set.
- Controls usable at minimum touch target size.
